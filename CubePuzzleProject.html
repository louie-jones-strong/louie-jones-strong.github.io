Alhambra 4D Cube Puzzle

After finishing collage I was invited to attempt to solve the Alhambra Puzzle.

What is the Alhambra Puzzle?

The puzzle is made of 8 unique cubes (the harder version has a extra cube). These cubes sub cubes are assembled together to form the mega cube. The sub cubes have a triangle on each of their faces, when the puzzle is assembled these triangles create a patten on the face of each of the faces of the mega cube. To check if the puzzle if solved all the faces on the mega cube need to match a set of allowed patten. However you also need to check the inside faces of the mega cube. This means that there are 12 faces on the mega cube that need to have the correct patten.
